About

Gayathri Furnitures

Gayathri Furniture has always been in the forefront to offer practical and innovative furniture solutions. With 2 decades of serving homes in Chennai and across Tamilnadu, we are still learning and evolving to deliver true customer satisfaction. Crowning the journey has been the recent renovations to our ECR showroom becoming the largest exclusive furniture showroom in Chennai. A continuous effort, but we know Furniture better!!
